Our Goal

The Goal we have for this app is to distribute it through the Driller Band Community, to help students with ease when accessing sites and the Band Sites, and that one day this app will be installed on the nice Kindles that were donated to the Band as a resource.

Our Inspiration

We just sat in a class one day, and we were like..."Why not build an App to help band people find stuff with the ease of one simple app?" That is how it started.

The App's Purpose on Your Device

Not only does this app give you access to synergy but it will give you access to the band sites which are a big thing when your a part of the Driller Band.

App's Quality

The app has a great layout and is easy to read, unlike most colored background things(like posters for an elementary school project) and will receive an occasional update when something needs fixing or when the overall looks of the app change.

Sponsors
